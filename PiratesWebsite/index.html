  • DU Pirate Party

    The aim and purpose of DU Pirates as outlined in our constitution is “To promote and raise awareness of the core values of freedom of information and culture, protection of civil liberties and individual privacy.”

    Our events range over a number of fields. From Open Discussions about topics like CopyRight, Open Source and freedom of information, the hotly debated topic in the EU, Article 11 and Article 13, that could revolutionize our internet forever, to fun social events like Treasure hunts, collaborations for pub quizzes and Capture the Flag competitions, workshops, and talk series, like our successful Security Series.
